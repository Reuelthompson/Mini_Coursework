Welcome to Asteroid Antics
You are in space trying to avoid asteroids while collecting space junk.
You have a limited ammount of fuel to work with to collect at least 5 pieces of space junk
To move in any direction use the W, A, S, & D keys and 'Q' to Jump 2 places (Uses 2 fuel).

Difficulty:
press '0' for easy mode.
press '1' for hard mode.
